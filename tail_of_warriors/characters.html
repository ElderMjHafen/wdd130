Characters Bios

Marlocks

A: 23

S: Male

C: Cavalier

W/MP: Long swords and natural weapons

Marlocks, or Marl for short is a resident of a city called Ulartus that was attacked by the Wildbringers who had forced prior victims to live as ferals. Being a survivor he is sent out confront the Wildbringers througout the providences and made cavalier. He is able cover ground rather quickly and effectively being Taur, he doesn't need a mount, he is the mount, although he typically will not allow others to use him as one.

Marl uses long swords and his legs when in combat. He has exellent hearing and reflexes making difficult and more dangerous for foes to attack him from behind.

Czarnox

A: 42

S: Male

C: Viscount

W/MP: Sword

One of the villains of the story, Czarnox desires to dethrone the free govenment of Veariaum and establish a king to replace the judges and cheif judge. He and his followers to establish elites over the providence, stiring anger to lure in more followers through conniving manipuations, decite, specious beguiling. He is one of the top leading members of the Monarchic Council who desire to put species against each other to create a desire to be control over and make them think the Free Order are the oppressors, when reality it is the other way around

Vaanhi

A: 16

S: Male

C: Equerry Squire

W/MP: Sword

In a neighboring providence Razkazca, Vaanhi was a street orphan that was taken in by the Razkazcan King as a equerry and as a equire. He was practically part of the royal family, until as he was stewarding for the royals, he stumpled upon a group conspiring a coup with Monarchic Council members from Veariaum against the royals to maintain the monarchy as the royals proposed to establish a cheif judge and judges as had Veariaum. Vaanhi tried to warn the royals about this conspiracy, but regardless of him try to go in cover to try to warn or he capture by the rebels, he failed and the kingdom fell under a new rule instead of having judges being establish. He fled from Razkazca to Veariaum and now become a young adventurer.
